International Supersonic Spectral wave Liverpool band

Here's a timeline of the band's history:

  • 1993 - Audun Laading is born in Vest-Agder, Norway
  • 1994 - Stephen Fitzpatrick is born in Barrow-in-Furness, England
  • 2013 - Fitzpatrick and Laading meet during their first year at the Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts
  • 2014 - The duo form the band Her's in jest and film a joke music video
  • 2015 - Her's transitions into an official band, start writing music, and perform their first gig at The SafeHouse MCR
  • 2016 - Her's sign to record label Heist or Hit, debut their first official song Dorothy and begin playing concerts
  • 2017 - Her's release a collection of singles entitled Songs of hers which is very well received
  • 2018 - Her's debut their first official full-length album Invitation to Her's following in the success of their previous work
  • 2019 - The Her's duo, along with their manager Trevor Engelbrekston, die while on their second tour of The United States.

"Her's are not a group to stand still. Each song they produce, from the lo-fi bedroom pop power of debut track to 'Dorothy' to the duo's equally melodic take on slacker-rock on 'Marcel', sees the Liverpool-based duo shift and float between sounds. Never really fitting into one genre, but somehow nailing whatever they end up doing."

-- NME writer Thomas Smith
